Instructions

You can customize your first fighter and second fighter names and races, you can choose up to 9 races that include both Heroes and Enemies.

You can start a battle by customizing your fighters and clicking the "Start game" button. Unlike the simulation, this mode allows you to manually start the battle, attack and restart the game if wanted to.

To start a simulation you need to customize both of your fighters and then click the "Run simulation 1vs1" button. Simulations are automatized fights between your heroes/enemies, wich means you won't be able to interact.

By clicking the "Run simulation 1vsAll" button, your first fighter will fight against every signle enemie of the game. Because of this, you won't need to customize your second fighter since it's a One VS All fight.
